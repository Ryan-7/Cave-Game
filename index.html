Weapon and Battle explanation

All weapons have three stats, power, accuracy and luck. Power is the amount of damage dealt to the enemy. Accuracy is the chance of hitting the enemy based on 10, so an accuracy of 6 means you have a 60% of hitting the enemy each turn. When you miss an enemy, the enemy always retaliates. So essentially, the accuracy system is also your defenese system. Luck indicates how often you will find the enemy's drop. This can range from gold to special items. It is also based on 10, a weapon with a luck of 9 has a 90% of obtaining the enemy's drop each kill.
